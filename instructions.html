FuelSharp NSW

Instructions

Welcome to FuelSharp NSW

This tool shows you real fuel prices at petrol stations across New South Wales. You can add your own discounts as well (and even see what the price would be if you paid in XDC or USDC!)

How to Use It

1

Choose your fuel type

At the top of the page, select U91, E10, Diesel, P95 or P98.

2

Move around the map

Zoom in and out or drag the map to look at different areas.

3

Check the list below the map

You'll see the cheapest stations currently visible on the map, sorted from cheapest to most expensive.

4

Add your discounts (recommended)

Scroll down to the Your Discounts section. Select a brand, enter how many cents cheaper you get, and save it. Your real price will then show in the list. (Note: Only service station brands currently displayed on the map will show in the dropdown).

Useful Features

  • • See your actual price after your discounts are applied
  • • View prices in XDC or USDC (great if you use crypto payment cards)
  • • Works great on your phone — just add it to your home screen

How It Works

FuelSharp gets its fuel prices directly from the NSW Government’s official database. This means the prices are accurate and up to date.

When you choose to see prices in XDC or USDC, the tool simply converts the dollar price using the current exchange rate. This is especially helpful if you use payment cards that let you spend XDC or stablecoins directly.

About XDC Network

This tool was built for the XDC Network — a fast and low-cost blockchain designed for real-world use. Showing prices in XDC and USDC makes it easy for people who already use crypto cards to see what fuel would actually cost them.
